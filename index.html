PT. MENARA INTI KARYA

INDONESIA

PT Menara Inti Karya (MIK) is a general construction company based in Industry Area Jababeka, Cikarang-Bekasi, Indonesia. Established in 2007, MIK has already built several projects in various areas in Indonesia, such as Jakarta, Cikarang, Makassar, Bekasi, Tangerang, and West Java.
